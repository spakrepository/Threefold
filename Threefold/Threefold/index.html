About Us

Perhaps it’s easier to say who we’re not. We’re not your archetypal agency, capable of thinking the thought, talking the walk but delegating the do. Nor are we your traditional production company, proficient at producing, but short of the strategic shizzle.

Nope, we’re neither, and yet we’re both...

A NEW BREED

A Hybrid Production House

That thinks and solves as much as it makes and produces. Born from a collective of people with a track record of designing ideas for any channel, but more importantly who then write, shoot, code and produce those ideas in-house, and work with smart clients and agencies to bring them to life.

A media mongrel some might say. One that lives and dies by the strength of its ideas and the height of their production values. For whom craft is never a compromise and a result is never an accident. Who believe “the line” is what Johnny Cash walked, not a marketing distinction, and that impossible is merely an opinion...not a conclusion.

Our Associates

Three Fold Productions is proud to be associated with the iconic directors for Television Commercials only.

Priyadarshan

Priyadarshan is an Indian film director, producer, and screenwriter. In a career spanning over three decades, he has directed more than 95 films in various Indian languages.

Saket Chaudhary

Saket Chaudhary is an Indian screenwriter and director. He co-wrote Santosh Sivan's Asoka and is the director of Pyaar Ke Side Effects, released in September 2006.

Soham Shah

Soham Shah is a Bollywood movie director who is popularly known for directing the films Kaal, one of the highest grossing Bollywood films of 2005, and the movie Luck.

Gaurav Bhatt

His works are easy to relate to and are distinguished as much by their clever, twisting techniques as their outbursts of extreme visual imagery.

Ashish R Mohan

Ashish R Mohan is an Indian/Foreign Film director and actor. His work as a director include Khiladi 786 and Welcome 2 Karachi. Ashish is from the city of Shimla, Himachal Pradesh.He was interested in film making since childhood.
